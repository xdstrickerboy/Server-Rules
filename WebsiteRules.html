FiveM Server Rules

General Rules

  • (1.1) Respect all players - Treat others with respect. Discrimination, hate speech, and harassment are strictly prohibited.
  • (1.2) No cheating, exploiting, or hacking - Using cheats, hacks, or exploiting game mechanics will result in a permanent ban.
  • (1.3) No Random Deathmatching (RDM) or Vehicle Deathmatching (VDM) - Killing another player without proper roleplay justification or using vehicles as weapons without valid proper roleplay justification is not allowed.
  • (1.4) No Meta-Gaming or Power-Gaming - Using information from external sources (Twitch, Discord, etc.) in-game is not allowed or Forcing actions upon another player or acting unrealistically to gain an advantage is prohibited.
  • (1.5) No Combat Logging - Disconnecting from the server to avoid roleplay or consequences is forbidden.

Roleplay Rules

  • (2.3) Characters Realsim - Characters must have realistic backstories and act accordingly. Unrealistic behavior is not permitted.
  • (2.3) Fear RP - You must fear for your life in realistic situations (e.g., comply when held at gunpoint).
  • (2.3) New Life Rule (NLR) - If you die, you forget the events leading to your death and cannot return to the scene.
  • (2.3) Emergency Services Priority - NHS, Police, and Fire/Rescue personnel must be respected in roleplay scenarios.

Vehicle & Aviation Rules

  • (3.1) No Unrealistic Driving - Speeding through the city with no consequences or crashing without injury RP is not allowed.
  • (3.2) Restricted Vehicles - Some vehicles (military, emergency, etc.) require proper permissions or roles.
  • (3.3) Airspace Regulations - Air traffic control (ATC) must be followed, and restricted airspace rules apply.

Emergency Services Rules

  • (4.1) Follow Chain of Command - If you are in EMS, Police, or Fire roles, respect your hierarchy.
  • (4.2) Proper Equipment Use - Only use tools and weapons that fit your job role.
  • (4.3) No Unrealistic Medical Revivals - Reviving must be roleplayed properly; you cannot self-revive unless authorized.

Criminal & Gang Rules

  • (5.1) Gang Limits - Gangs must have a maximum number of members (4) and be registered with staff.
  • (5.2) No Unrealistic Heists - Robberies and heists must be properly planned and executed within roleplay.
  • (5.3) No Cop Hunting - Do not engage in violence against law enforcement without RP justification.

Admin & Staff Rules

  • (6.1) Follow Admin Instructions - Admins have the final say in disputes and rule enforcement.
  • (6.2) No Admin Impersonation - Pretending to be an admin or staff member will result in a ban.
  • (6.3) Report Rule Breakers Properly - Use the correct channels (Discord, ticket system) to report issues.

Streaming & Content Creation

  • (7.1) Follow Community Guidelines - Content creators must follow all server rules while streaming or recording.
  • (7.2) No Stream Sniping - Using someone's stream to gain an in-game advantage is prohibited.

Punishments & Bans

  • (8.1) Strike System - Repeated rule violations will result in escalating punishments, including warnings, kicks, temporary bans, and permanent bans.
  • (8.2) Appeal Process - If you believe a punishment was unfair, you may appeal through the proper Discord or forum channels.

Communication Rules

  • (9.1) In-Character (IC) & Out-of-Character (OOC) Separation - Do not use OOC chat to influence in-game situations.
  • (9.2) Voice & Text Roleplay - If using voice chat, stay in character at all times. If using text, maintain proper RP formatting.
  • (9.3) No Mic Spam - Do not play loud music or spam voice chat with unnecessary noise.
